Our research group, Intelligent Aerial Robotics, has been actively engaged in Bio-inspired Vision and Control, Reinforcement Learning, Advanced Flight Control, Deep Learning-based Control, Micro Air Vehicles (MAVs) Swarm for the past year. Situated within the dynamic environment of Unmanned Aerial Vehicle Laboratory (UAV Lab) of Universiti Sains Malaysia, our team is dedicated to achieving autonomous, intelligent aerial robots.
